About Me

My name is Adrian Moravec. I will be graduating from the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at the University of Minnesota in July 2019. I am familiar and comfortable using a wide range of different technologies including, but not limited to: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, Express, Handlebars, SQL, and MongoDB.

I grew up in a small town of 6,000 in Nebraska and moved to attend school in Minneapolis. I graduated from Augsburg College with a double major in Music and Film. I then went on to study and obtain a Master's Degree in Film Journalism at the University of Scotland.

I have two lovely (if aggravatingly difficult to put to sleep) children aged 2 and 5 and been married for twelve years. After working various retail jobs I currently teach private piano and guitar lessons at Beacon Academy in Crystal.

I currently reside in Brooklyn Park, MN where I watch as many movies as I possibly can and try to find time to cook and bake (particularly cookies).
